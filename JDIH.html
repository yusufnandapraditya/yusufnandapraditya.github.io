Law Information Mobile App

The Law Information app provides easy access to Yogyakarta’s regional laws and legal documents. Users can quickly search, read, and download official documents. It supports legal transparency and accessibility for the public.

Problem

Before the mobile version, access to legal documents was limited to the desktop website and physical archives. This led to several key issues:

  • Searching for legal documents was slow and inefficient.
  • General users struggled to understand legal terminology and structure.

Project Goals

  • Simplify public access to regional legal documents.
  • Promote transparency and openness of public legal information.
  • Support the digital transformation of regional government legal services.
  • Improve public legal literacy through an intuitive and informative user interface.

Project Timeline

Target Audience

  • Government Officials & Civil Servants: Need fast access for drafting and referencing regulations.
  • Academics & Legal Researchers: Require verified legal data for study and publication.
  • General Public: Seek simplified and accessible legal information.
  • Legal Practitioners & Consultants: Need powerful search and download features for professional use.
